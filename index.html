Introduction

The Going Home Club - A club for people that have no intention of joining a club. Hence the “going home” part.
There’s no way such a club actually exists, right?

It does exist!?

You would be forgiven if you took a quick glance at the title, poster, and synopsis and thought that this was just another school centered show featuring girls doing ordinary things. It does mostly take place in a school, however, the things that the girls of the Going Home Club do are not what you would normally see in the normal school comedy show. In fact, the show very well could be considered quite different from the usual school comedy show with the complete disregard for the 4th wall with plenty of meta references that viewers with knowledge of the current topic can nod and say, “hey, I know what they’re talking about!”
